About Rainbow Reader

A Magical World of Learning for Your Kids

Rainbow Reader was born from a simple passion: making learning a magical adventure for every child. We believe creativity and imagination are the keys to harmonious development.

Our collection of coloring books, kindergarten workbooks, and activity books is carefully crafted by passionate educators and illustrators. Every page is an invitation to explore, learn, and have fun.
